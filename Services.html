Our comprehensive services are designed to support survivors of violence at every stage of their healing journey.


Safe Houses:


Our safe houses offer secure and confidential spaces for survivors to find refuge and begin the healing process. Each facility is equipped with essential resources, creating a nurturing environment conducive to recovery.

Counselling and Therapy:


We provide trauma-informed counselling and therapy to help survivors address their emotional and psychological needs. Our services include individual and group sessions designed to support survivors in navigating their healing journey.

Legal Aid:

Our legal aid services assist survivors in navigating the legal system, from documentation to court support. We ensure that survivors have access to justice and legal resources.


Skills Training and Empowerment:

Empowerment is at the core of our mission. We offer vocational training, life skills education, and empowerment workshops to help survivors build self-sufficiency and independence.

Mission:


Haven of Hope is dedicated to providing a safe haven and comprehensive support services to survivors of gender-based violence (GBV), empowering them to reclaim their independence and rebuild their lives. We envision a world where GBV is eradicated, and every individual can live free from fear.


Our Story:

Founded to address the increasing crisis of GBV in South Africa, Haven of Hope serves as a vital support system for those impacted. We don't just offer shelter; we pave the way for recovery and a brighter future.

Welcome to Haven of Hope, where we are committed to providing a secure haven and comprehensive assistance to survivors of gender-based violence (GBV) in South Africa. Our goal is to empower individuals to heal, rebuild, and flourish. If you or someone you know requires immediate assistance, we are here to help.
